Many people will balance car and bicycle confused, balance car is no car chain and foot, completely rely on the baby to slide forward, is a good transition product before riding a bicycle. The balance car mainly exercises the baby's balance ability, belongs to the baby's first mobility car, beginners just set foot on the balance car will appear very unstable, but this belongs to the normal phenomenon, because the baby's balance is weak, so it can not control the body balance well. Need baby feet on the ground, both legs together to slide forward, exercise the balanced development of baby's legs muscles. Balance car in exercise balance at the same time can also exercise the baby's confidence, the more dare to contact the outside world, the baby's independent ability will be more helpful. Balancing bikes are more like extreme sports for children, where babies can ride freely and confidently in full control of the bike. So the balanced car will make the baby more likely to feel a sense of security, easier to drive, higher security; More importantly, they can enjoy the sense of accomplishment of cycling and enjoy outdoor sports more.


Matters needing attention

One is to see the sign, must pass the national "CCC" certification, including the product name, enterprise information, the main material, age and other elements, do not buy "three no" products; Second, for appearance, don't buy toys with small parts that can be swallowed or inhaled, as well as parts with tips or rough edges. Three is to see the design, the wheel and the frame can not have a dangerous crack, to prevent lively and active children accidentally reach into the clamp. Be sure to wear a helmet when riding children's balance bike outdoors to protect the safety of baby's head. Meanwhile, beginners are advised to wear gloves, knee pads, elbow pads and other protective equipment. When riding, you must be accompanied by adults. You are not allowed to ride on public roads or near dangerous areas such as water, steps and cliffs.
